Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any hidden monthly fees?

No hidden agency fees. The price you see is what you pay us for the build. However, you will need to pay standard yearly fees to your domain registrar (like GoDaddy) and a web host (like GitHub or Hostinger) to keep your site on the internet.

What if I need changes after launch?

We offer a 14-day grace period for minor text and image tweaks for free! For major overhauls or new page additions down the line, we charge a simple, transparent hourly rate.

How fast can you build my site?

If you choose the Starter package and have your images and text ready, we can often launch your site within 72 hours. The Growth package typically takes about a week to perfect the custom animations and pages.

Do I need to provide the photos?

Yes, for the best results, you should provide high-quality photos of your specific business, food, or products. If you don't have any yet, we can temporarily use premium, royalty-free stock images that fit your brand.
